Model Comparison

This page explains the models used in the gait classifier. Click Analyze to generate the confusion matrices, accuracy/loss curves, classification report, and comparison table.

CNN

The CNN is a custom convolutional model trained directly on gait silhouette images. It learns local shape patterns from the walking silhouette and works as the baseline model.

ResNet / MobileNet

This slot represents transfer learning. Since the notebook saved MobileNet instead of ResNet, the app uses MobileNet as the fallback transfer-learning comparison model.

EfficientNetB0

EfficientNetB0 is a transfer-learning model. It compares the custom CNN against a pretrained architecture adapted for gait silhouette classification.
